Bed Bugs Profile

Overview: Cimex lectularius

Bed bugs hide inside mattress seams, bed frames, baseboards, and luggage. They feed on human blood at night, causing skin irritation and severe mental distress. They travel on clothing and bags, making hotels and multi-family structures high-risk centers.

Threat Level High (Bite / Insomnia)
Preferred Nesting Mattress Tufts & Frames
Active Cycles Year-Round (Travel Peak)

Key Nesting Indications

Look for dark rust-colored blood spots on bed sheets, tiny translucent skin casings, and groupings of red, itchy bites arranged in lines on skin.

Professional Treatment Options

Because bed bugs have developed extreme resistance to synthetic chemical sprays, we recommend 130°F (54°C) thermal remediation to neutralize bugs and eggs in a single visit.
