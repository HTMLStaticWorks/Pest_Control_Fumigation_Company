Signs of Damage

Is Your Property Harboring Termites?

Termites eat structural cellulose wood 24 hours a day, causing billions in uninsured home damage annually. Detecting colony activity early is vital to saving your home's framing structure.

  • Mud Tubes: Dirt lines climbing concrete foundations.
  • Hollow Drywood: Structural wood that sounds hollow when tapped.
  • Swarming Wings: Piles of discarded wings near windows or doors.
