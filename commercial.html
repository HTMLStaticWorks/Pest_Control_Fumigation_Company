Integrated Pest Management (IPM) & Regulatory Compliance

For industries like food logistics and medical production, pest activity triggers immediate compliance failures. Our IPM standard isolates the root causes of nesting using ecological adjustments instead of simply blanketing areas in chemicals.

We supply complete digital documentation dashboards including operator license logs, active materials data sheets (SDS), mapping coordinates for all traps, and trending logs ready for inspections by the health department, AIB, SQF, or USDA.
