Termite Control & Baiting

Liquid Soil Barriers, Baiting Networks & Scent-Dogs

Subterranean termites cause billions of dollars in structural damage annually. Our professional termite mitigation program uses advanced soil treatments, bait systems, and thermal barriers to eradicate termite colonies at the source before they compromise your structure.

We employ highly trained scent-dog detection teams to locate active infestations inside wall voids. Once mapped, we apply liquid soil barriers along foundations and embed continuous monitoring bait grids to ensure complete exclusion and protection, backed by our $1 Million structural damage warranty.

Treatment Timeline Details

1

Scent-Dog Detection & Mapping

Deploying certified termite canine teams to locate nests behind walls, subfloors, and structures with pinpoint accuracy.

2

Fluid Soil Injections

Applying liquid barriers directly into soil trenches along the foundation line to establish an invisible defensive shield.

3

Baiting Network Installation

Embedding continuous monitoring bait stations around the property perimeter, inspected regularly by our technicians.
